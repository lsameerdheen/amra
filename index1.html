Amra Research Consultancy and Solutions is vibrant organization focused at providing fast, reliable and accurate testing of broadest range of materials. We provide a wider range of testing and material characterization services. Its professional staff has years of materials science experience in X-Ray diffraction techniques, chemical analysis, metallographic analysis, failure analysis, particle size analysis, high resolution image and thermal analysis.
