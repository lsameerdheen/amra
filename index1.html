It is structure that we look for whenever we try to understand anything. All science is built upon this search; we investigate how crystals aggregate; how atoms are fastened together; how electrons constitute a chemical bond between atoms; how the cell is built of reticular material, cytoplasm, chromosomes. We like to understand, and to explain, observed facts in terms of structure. A chemist who understands why a diamond has certain properties, or why nylon or hemoglobin have other properties, because of the different ways their atoms are arranged, may ask questions that a geologist would not think of formulating, unless he had been similarly trained in this way of thinking about the world. — Linus Pauling

Our Portfolio

AMRA Research Consultancy and Services is vibrant organization focused at providing fast, reliable and accurate testing of broadest range of materials having micro-, meso- and nanoscale structures. We have experienced materials scientists, metallurgists, chemists and engineers having expertise in specialized areas of materials testing, analysis and advanced characterization. Our comprehensive selection of testing and inspection services includes metallographic services, failure analysis, compositional analysis and material identification, high resolution imaging of microstructure, thermal analysis, advanced microscopy techniques of nanoparticles, alloys and metals, composites, ceramics, polymers, minerals and thin films at a competitive cost with the technical expertise and a cooperative approach to our clients. Additionally, we work on consultation, knowledge transfer, professional interpretation of results and recommendations for further action. AMRA research consultancy have emerged to provide research services not only to the researchers but also to industries for qualification of their raw materials and products to help create new solutions that redefine the limits of performance at an affordable price. AMRA is an ultimate solution for all your research needs which provides accurate and reliable data to its customers.
